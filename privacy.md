PRIVACY POLICY

1. Introduction

   VibeCore is committed to protecting the privacy of users of A Strange Game.
   This policy transparently explains how the application handles (or rather
   does not handle) your personal data.

2. Data collected

   A Strange Game does not collect, store, transmit or share any personal data.
   The application does not access any information that could be used to
   identify a user.

   In particular, the application:
   - Does not collect your name, email address or any other identifier.
   - Does not access your geographic location.
   - Does not access your camera, microphone, photo gallery or contacts.
   - Does not read your personal files.
   - Does not use cookies or tracking technologies.
   - Does not send any data to a remote server.

3. Offline operation

   A Strange Game is a fully offline game. No Internet connection is required
   or established during use of the application.

4. Third-party services

   The application does not integrate any third-party services (advertising,
   analytics, social networks, in-app purchases, etc.). No third-party SDK
   that could collect data is used.

5. Children's data

   A Strange Game collects no data and is suitable for all ages. No personal
   information is gathered from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

6. Changes to this policy

   Any future changes to this policy will be published at the same address and,
   if the changes are significant, announced in application updates on the
   Play Store.

7. Contact

   For any questions regarding this privacy policy, you may contact the
   developer at the following address:
   contact.vibecore@proton.me
